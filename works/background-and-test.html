My Constrast

Contrast occurs when two or more visual elements in a composition are different. In design we use contrast to generate impact, highlight importance, create exciting graphics and create visual interest and dynamics.

Context is integral to contrast. We may think that the chosen visual object in a composition says something about itself but it is more often the visual elements around it that give it its meaning.

You say by contrast or in contrast, or in contrast to something, to show that you are mentioning a very different situation from the one you have just mentioned.
